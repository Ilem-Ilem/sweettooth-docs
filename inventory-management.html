Items Management

Overview

The Items Management module allows you to create, update, and maintain your inventory items with detailed specifications.

Key Features:

  • Create new inventory items with SKU, category, and unit of measure
  • Set reorder levels and maximum stock levels for automated alerts
  • Track item status (active/inactive) and health
  • Manage stock adjustments with approval workflows
  • View item history and related transactions

Access & Permissions:

Users with "Inventory Manager" or "Stock Controller" roles can access this module.

Important: Items with low stock levels will appear in the dashboard alerts. Regular monitoring helps prevent stockouts.

Purchases

Overview

The Purchases module handles procurement activities, from creating purchase orders to receiving goods and updating stock levels.

Key Features:

  • Create purchase orders with multiple items and suppliers
  • Track purchase status (draft, pending approval, approved)
  • Record supplier information and contact details
  • Calculate landed costs including shipping and other fees
  • Automatic stock updates upon purchase approval
  • Integration with approval workflows for better control

Workflow:

  1. Create purchase order with items and quantities
  2. Submit for approval if required by role permissions
  3. Wait for approval from authorized personnel
  4. Receive goods and update stock levels automatically
  5. Track payment status (paid, partial, pending)

Stock Levels

Overview

The Stock Levels module provides real-time visibility into your inventory with detailed tracking of available, reserved, and damaged stock.

Key Features:

  • Real-time stock tracking with available, reserved, and damaged quantities
  • Health status monitoring (good, warning, critical, expired)
  • Automatic reorder level alerts when stock falls below threshold
  • Stock adjustment capabilities with approval workflows
  • Historical stock movement tracking
  • Integration with purchasing for automated reordering

Stock Categories:

  • Available: Stock ready for use or sale
  • Reserved: Stock allocated for specific purposes
  • Damaged: Stock that is unusable due to damage
  • Total: Sum of all stock categories

Health Checks

Overview

The Health Checks module allows you to monitor and assess the condition of your inventory items to ensure quality and safety.

Key Features:

  • Record condition assessments for inventory items
  • Document observations and required actions
  • Track quantity affected by health issues
  • Monitor expiration dates and shelf life
  • Generate health reports and trends
  • Integrate with stock management for automatic updates

Health Conditions:

  • Good: Item in excellent condition
  • Fair: Item usable but showing signs of wear
  • Poor: Item needs attention or repair
  • Damaged: Item is damaged but may be repairable
  • Expired: Item has exceeded its shelf life

Item Requests

Overview

The Item Requests module facilitates the process of requesting inventory items from the store by different departments.

Key Features:

  • Submit requests for inventory items by department
  • Specify quantities and required dates
  • Track request status (pending, approved, fulfilled)
  • Link requests to specific production shifts or projects
  • Integration with approval workflows
  • Automatic stock reservation upon approval

Request Process:

  1. Department creates item request with required items and quantities
  2. Request is submitted for approval
  3. Inventory team reviews and approves the request
  4. Items are reserved from stock
  5. Items are dispatched to requesting department
View Detailed Documentation

Dispatches

Overview

The Dispatches module handles the process of releasing inventory items from the store to requesting departments.

Key Features:

  • Process approved item requests for dispatch
  • Update stock levels automatically upon dispatch
  • Track dispatched quantities and remaining balances
  • Link dispatches to specific shifts or projects
  • Maintain audit trail of all dispatch activities
  • Generate dispatch reports and summaries

Dispatch Process:

  1. Select approved item request for dispatch
  2. Verify available stock for requested items
  3. Update quantities and adjust stock levels
  4. Record dispatch details and responsible person
  5. Update request status to partially or fully dispatched

Callbacks

Overview

The Callbacks module handles the process of returning unused raw materials and managing finished product rejects from production.

Key Features:

  • Record raw material returns from production
  • Handle finished product rejects and quality issues
  • Process callbacks with inventory approval
  • Update stock levels based on callback outcomes
  • Track callback reasons and patterns
  • Generate callback reports for analysis

Callback Types:

  • Raw Material Return: Unused materials returned from production
  • Finished Product Reject: Defective products returned from production
