Prep Work

Overview

Prep Work involves preparing ingredients and materials in advance to support production staff.

Ingredient Preparation:

  • Measure and portion ingredients according to recipes
  • Pre-cut vegetables, fruits, and other ingredients
  • Thaw frozen ingredients as needed
  • Prepare marinades and sauces in advance
  • Sort and organize ingredients by production schedule
  • Label and date all prepared ingredients

Equipment Setup:

  • Prepare workstations with necessary tools
  • Set up equipment according to production needs
  • Ensure all equipment is clean and functional
  • Stock work areas with supplies and materials
  • Arrange ingredients in logical order for workflow
  • Prepare backup equipment as needed

Organization:

  • Arrange ingredients by preparation sequence
  • Organize tools and utensils for easy access
  • Set up mise en place for anticipated production
  • Ensure work areas are clean and sanitized
  • Verify all necessary items are available
  • Communicate preparation status to production staff

Tip: Thorough prep work significantly improves production efficiency and workflow.

Cleaning

Overview

Cleaning maintains equipment and workspace cleanliness to ensure food safety and hygiene.

Equipment Cleaning:

  • Disassemble equipment according to cleaning protocols
  • Scrub all surfaces with appropriate cleaning solutions
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all cleaning residue
  • Sanitize equipment with approved sanitizers
  • Reassemble equipment following safety procedures
  • Verify equipment functionality after cleaning

Workspace Sanitation:

  • Clean and sanitize all work surfaces
  • Empty and clean sinks and drains
  • Sanitize cutting boards and utensils
  • Clean floors with appropriate solutions
  • Sanitize door handles and touchpoints
  • Restock cleaning supplies as needed

Safety Protocols:

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment
  • Follow chemical safety guidelines
  • Ensure proper ventilation during cleaning
  • Store cleaning chemicals safely away from food
  • Dispose of waste materials properly
  • Document cleaning activities as required

Best Practice: Consistent cleaning practices are essential for food safety and regulatory compliance.

Inventory Support

Overview

Inventory Support assists with monitoring stock levels and organizing materials.

Stock Monitoring:

  • Check inventory levels at regular intervals
  • Identify low-stock items that need replenishment
  • Report out-of-stock ingredients to supervisors
  • Verify expiration dates on all items
  • Rotate stock using first-in-first-out principle
  • Document inventory discrepancies

Organization:

  • Arrange items by category and frequency of use
  • Ensure proper storage conditions for different ingredients
  • Label shelves and storage areas clearly
  • Keep walkways clear and organized
  • Secure valuable or restricted items appropriately
  • Maintain cleanliness in storage areas

Receiving Support:

  • Assist with receiving deliveries
  • Verify delivered items against purchase orders
  • Check quality and condition of received goods
  • Store items in appropriate locations
  • Document any delivery discrepancies
  • Report damaged or spoiled items immediately

Important: Accurate inventory support prevents production delays and waste.

Order Assistance

Overview

Order Assistance helps fulfill production orders under supervision of senior staff.

Order Processing:

  • Receive and understand order requirements
  • Identify necessary ingredients and equipment
  • Follow standardized procedures for preparation
  • Work under supervision of experienced staff
  • Complete tasks within specified timeframes
  • Report progress to supervising staff

Quality Standards:

  • Follow recipes and procedures precisely
  • Meet established quality standards
  • Seek guidance when uncertain about procedures
  • Report any quality issues immediately
  • Participate in quality checks
  • Learn from feedback and corrections

Efficiency:

  • Work efficiently without compromising quality
  • Minimize waste during production
  • Keep work areas organized during tasks
  • Anticipate needs of production staff
  • Communicate potential delays proactively
  • Complete assigned tasks thoroughly

Tip: Effective order assistance supports production efficiency while maintaining quality standards.
