Leave Balance

The Leave Balance page provides a comprehensive overview of your available leave days, usage history, and remaining entitlements for the selected year.

Accessing Leave Balance

  1. Log in to the system with your employee credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Leave" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. Click on "Leave Balance" from the submenu
  5. The Leave Balance page will display your current leave allocations

Year Selection

The system allows you to view leave balances for different years:

Year Selector:

  • Year Dropdown - (dropdown) Select the year you want to view (typically current year and adjacent years)
  • The default view shows the current year's leave balance
  • Changing the year updates all displayed information to reflect that year's data

Year Selection Impact:

  • Leave allocations are year-specific
  • Used days are tracked per year
  • Remaining days are calculated based on the selected year
  • Leave history reflects the selected year

Leave Balance Overview

The page displays a summary of your leave entitlements:

Summary Statistics:

  • Total Allocated - Total number of leave days allocated to you for the selected year
  • Total Used - Number of leave days you have already taken
  • Total Pending - Number of leave days in pending applications
  • Total Remaining - Number of leave days still available to you

Calculation Formula:

Total Remaining = Total Allocated - Total Used - Total Pending

Leave Type Breakdown

The page displays detailed information for each leave type:

Per-Leave-Type Information:

  • Leave Type Name - The name of the leave category (e.g., Annual Leave, Sick Leave)
  • Total Allocated - Days allocated for this specific leave type
  • Used Days - Days already taken from this leave type
  • Pending Days - Days in pending applications for this leave type
  • Remaining Days - Days still available for this leave type

Visual Representation:

  • Color-coded bars or charts may show the utilization percentage for each leave type
  • Icons or indicators may highlight low balance situations

Leave History

The page includes a history of your leave usage for the selected year:

History Information:

  • Leave Type - The type of leave taken
  • Dates - Start and end dates of the leave period
  • Days - Number of working days taken
  • Status - Current status of the leave application
  • Application Date - When the leave was applied for

History Sorting:

  • History is typically sorted by date, with most recent entries first
  • You can review patterns of leave usage over the year

Understanding Your Leave Balance

Key concepts to understand:

Available Balance:

  • This represents days you can immediately request
  • Does not include pending applications
  • May vary by leave type

Pending Applications:

  • Days in applications that are awaiting approval
  • These are temporarily deducted from your available balance
  • If approved, they become used days
  • If rejected, they return to your available balance

Leave Allocation:

  • Determined by your employment contract and company policy
  • May be adjusted by HR administrators for special circumstances
  • Typically resets at the beginning of each year

Tips and Best Practices

  • Regularly check your leave balance before planning time off
  • Review your leave history to understand usage patterns
  • Plan leave requests to avoid exceeding your available balance
  • Consider spreading leave throughout the year rather than taking it all at once
  • Be aware of company policies regarding carry-over of unused leave
  • Check different leave types as they may have separate allocations
  • Factor in pending applications when planning new requests
  • Consult with HR if you have questions about your allocation
  • Keep track of important deadlines for using allocated leave
