Apply Leave

The Apply Leave form allows employees to submit leave requests with all necessary details, including leave type, dates, reason, and supporting documentation.

Accessing Apply Leave

  1. Log in to the system with your employee credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Leave" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. Click on "Apply Leave" from the submenu
  5. The Apply Leave form will load with available leave types

Form Fields Overview

The Apply Leave form contains several sections with required and optional fields:

Required Information:

  • Leave Type - (dropdown) Select the type of leave you're requesting (e.g., Annual Leave, Sick Leave, etc.)
  • Start Date - (date picker) Select the first day of your leave (must be today or in the future)
  • End Date - (date picker) Select the last day of your leave (must be after start date)
  • Reason - (textarea) Provide a detailed explanation for your leave request (minimum 10 characters)

Optional Information:

  • Emergency Contact - (text input) Provide contact information for emergencies during your leave
  • Supporting Document - (file upload) Upload relevant documents if required by the leave type (PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, max 2MB)

Automatically Calculated Information:

  • Total Days - (calculated) Number of working days (excluding weekends) in your requested period
  • Available Balance - (calculated) Your remaining leave days for the selected leave type in the current year

Applying for Leave

Follow these steps to submit a leave request:

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Select the appropriate leave type from the dropdown
  2. Verify your available balance for the selected leave type
  3. Choose your start date (must be today or in the future)
  4. Choose your end date (must be after start date)
  5. Review the calculated total days to ensure they match your needs
  6. Provide a detailed reason for your leave (minimum 10 characters required)
  7. Enter emergency contact information if applicable
  8. Upload supporting documents if required by the leave type
  9. Click the "Submit" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface)

What Happens After Submission:

  • Your leave application is submitted for approval
  • The status changes to "Pending" until reviewed by a manager
  • Your available balance is temporarily reduced by the requested days
  • You can view the status of your application in "My Leaves"
  • You will receive notifications about approval or rejection

Leave Day Calculation

The system calculates leave days based on working days:

Calculation Method:

  • Only weekdays (Monday-Friday) are counted toward leave days
  • Weekends (Saturday and Sunday) are excluded from the calculation
  • Public holidays may or may not be excluded depending on system configuration
  • The total is displayed in real-time as you select dates

Example:

If you select a period from Monday to Friday (5 calendar days), it counts as 5 working days. If you select a period from Friday to Monday (4 calendar days), it counts as 2 working days.

Leave Type Requirements

Different leave types may have specific requirements:

Common Requirements:

  • Minimum Notice Period - Some leave types require advance notice (e.g., 7 days for annual leave)
  • Supporting Documents - Certain leave types require documentation (e.g., medical certificate for sick leave)
  • Maximum Consecutive Days - Some leave types limit the number of consecutive days that can be taken
  • Approval Requirements - Some leave types may require special approval processes

Validation Checks:

The system performs several validation checks before submission:

  • Ensures sufficient leave balance is available
  • Verifies minimum notice period is met
  • Confirms required documents are attached if needed
  • Validates that the requested period doesn't exceed maximum consecutive days

Validation and Error Handling

The form validates all required fields before submission:

Required Field Validation:

  • Leave type must be selected
  • Start date must be today or in the future
  • End date must be after start date
  • Reason must be provided with at least 10 characters

Common Error Messages:

  • "Insufficient leave balance" - You don't have enough days available for the selected leave type
  • "Minimum notice period required" - You applied for leave with insufficient advance notice
  • "Supporting document is required" - A document is required for the selected leave type but wasn't provided
  • "Maximum consecutive days exceeded" - Your request exceeds the allowed consecutive days for this leave type

Tips and Best Practices

  • Plan your leave well in advance to meet minimum notice requirements
  • Check your leave balance before applying to ensure sufficient days are available
  • Provide detailed reasons to help managers understand your request
  • Attach required documents to prevent delays in processing
  • Consider business needs and peak periods when requesting leave
  • Inform your team members about your upcoming leave
  • Complete urgent tasks before your leave starts
  • Arrange coverage for your responsibilities during your absence
  • Keep track of your leave applications in "My Leaves"
