My Leaves

The My Leaves page provides a comprehensive view of all your leave applications, including their current status, dates, and details. This page helps you track your leave history and manage pending requests.

Accessing My Leaves

  1. Log in to the system with your employee credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Leave" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. Click on "My Leaves" from the submenu
  5. The My Leaves page will display all your leave applications

Leave List Overview

The page displays a table with the following information for each leave application:

Table Columns:

  • Application # - Unique identifier for the leave application
  • Leave Type - The type of leave requested (e.g., Annual Leave, Sick Leave)
  • Leave Dates - The start and end dates of the requested leave
  • Days - The number of working days requested
  • Status - Current status of the application (Pending, Approved, Rejected, Cancelled)
  • Actions - Available actions for each application

Status Types:

  • Pending - Application submitted but not yet reviewed
  • Approved - Application approved by a manager
  • Rejected - Application denied by a manager
  • Cancelled - Application cancelled by the employee

Filtering and Searching

Use the filtering options to find specific leave applications:

Status Filter:

  • Status Dropdown - (dropdown) Filter by specific status (All, Pending, Approved, Rejected, Cancelled)

Search Options:

  • Search Box - (text input) Enter text to search across application numbers, reasons, and leave type names

Managing Leave Applications

Viewing Leave Details:

  1. Find the leave application in the list
  2. Click the "View Details" button (the button may appear as text, an eye icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A modal will appear with comprehensive details about the application
  4. Details include: application number, leave type, dates, reason, emergency contact, and supporting document

Cancelling a Leave Application:

  1. Find the leave application in the list (only pending or approved applications can be cancelled)
  2. Click the "Cancel" button (the button may appear as text, a cancel icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A modal will appear asking for a cancellation reason
  4. Enter a detailed reason for cancellation (minimum 10 characters required)
  5. Click the "Confirm" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to cancel the application
  6. The application status will change to "Cancelled"

Leave Details Modal

When viewing leave details, you'll see:

Application Information:

  • Application Number - Unique identifier for the application
  • Leave Type - The type of leave requested
  • Dates - Start and end dates of the requested leave
  • Total Days - Number of working days requested
  • Reason - Explanation provided for the leave request
  • Emergency Contact - Contact information provided for emergencies

Supporting Documents:

  • If a supporting document was uploaded, a "Download Document" button (the button may appear as text, a download icon, or similar depending on your interface) will be available
  • Click the button to download the document to your computer

Approval Information:

  • If approved: Approver name and approval date
  • If rejected: Rejection reason and date
  • If cancelled: Cancellation reason and date

Pagination

The leave list is divided into pages to improve performance. Use the pagination controls at the bottom of the table to navigate between pages. You can also adjust the number of leave applications displayed per page using the quantity selector.

Tips and Best Practices

  • Regularly check the status of your leave applications
  • Cancel leave applications promptly if your plans change
  • Provide detailed reasons when cancelling to help with planning
  • Keep track of approved leave dates to avoid scheduling conflicts
  • Review your leave history to plan future requests
  • Ensure all required documents are attached before submitting
  • Plan leave requests well in advance to meet notice requirements
  • Inform your team about approved leave dates
  • Arrange coverage for your responsibilities during your absence
