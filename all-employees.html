All Employees

The All Employees page provides a comprehensive view of all employees in your organization, allowing you to manage employee records, assign roles, and perform various administrative tasks.

Accessing All Employees

  1. Log in to the system with appropriate credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Employees" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. The All Employees page will display a list of employees in your branch

Employee List Overview

The employee list displays the following information for each employee:

  • Employee # - Unique identifier for the employee
  • Name - Full name of the employee
  • Email - Contact email address
  • Department - Department the employee belongs to
  • Status - Employment status (active, inactive, terminated, etc.)
  • Hire Date - Date the employee was hired
  • Salary - Employee's salary information
  • Roles - Assigned roles and permissions

Actions Column:

Each employee row includes an Actions column with the following options:

  • View Details - (the button may appear as text, an eye icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to view comprehensive employee information
  • Edit - (the button may appear as text, a pencil icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to modify employee information
  • Assign Roles - (the button may appear as text, a user-group icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to assign or modify employee roles
  • Delete - (the button may appear as text, a trash icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to remove the employee from the system

Filtering and Searching

Use the filtering options to find specific employees:

Quick Search:

  • Search Box - Enter text to search across employee names, emails, and employee numbers

Advanced Search:

  • Advanced Search - Search across multiple fields including name, email, employee number, phone, branch, and department

Filter Options:

  • Status Filter - Filter by employment status (active, inactive, terminated, on probation, on leave)
  • Department Filter - Filter by specific department
  • Branch Filter - Filter by specific branch
  • Gender Filter - Filter by gender
  • Shift Preference - Filter by shift preference (morning, afternoon, night, rotating, flexible)
  • Hire Date Range - Filter by hire date range
  • Termination Date Range - Filter by termination date range
  • Date Range - Filter by creation date range

Controls:

  • Apply Filters - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to apply all selected filters
  • Reset Filters - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to clear all filters and return to default view

Managing Employees

Viewing Employee Details:

  1. Find the employee in the list
  2. Click the "View Details" button (the button may appear as text, an eye icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A detailed view of the employee's information will be displayed

Editing Employee Information:

  1. Find the employee in the list
  2. Click the "Edit" button (the button may appear as text, a pencil icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. Modify the employee information as needed
  4. Click the "Save" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to save changes
  5. Note: Non-super-admin users may need to submit changes for approval

Assigning Roles:

  1. Find the employee in the list
  2. Click the "Assign Roles" button (the button may appear as text, a user-group icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A modal will appear with available roles
  4. Select the roles you want to assign to the employee
  5. Click the "Save" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to assign the roles
  6. Note: Non-super-admin users may need to provide a reason for role changes

Deleting an Employee:

  1. Find the employee in the list
  2. Click the "Delete" button (the button may appear as text, a trash icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you're sure you want to delete the employee
  4. Click "Confirm" to delete the employee or "Cancel" to keep them
  5. Note: Non-super-admin users must provide a reason for deletion, which requires approval

Bulk Operations

Perform actions on multiple employees simultaneously:

Selecting Multiple Employees:

  • Check the checkbox in the first column of each employee row you want to select
  • Or use the "Select All" checkbox to select all employees on the current page

Bulk Delete:

  1. Select multiple employees using the checkboxes
  2. Choose "Delete Selected" from the "Bulk Actions" dropdown
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear with the count of selected employees
  4. Click "Confirm" to delete all selected employees or "Cancel" to keep them
  5. Note: Bulk operations follow the same approval workflow as individual operations

Exporting Employee Data

Export employee information to CSV format:

Export All Filtered Employees:

  1. Apply any desired filters to narrow down the employee list
  2. Click the "Export Excel" button (the button may appear as text, a download icon, or similar depending on your interface)
  3. The system will generate a CSV file containing all filtered employees
  4. The file will automatically download to your computer

Export Selected Employees:

  1. Select specific employees using the checkboxes
  2. Choose "Export Selected" from the "Bulk Actions" dropdown
  3. The system will generate a CSV file containing only the selected employees
  4. The file will automatically download to your computer

Pagination

The employee list is divided into pages to improve performance. Use the pagination controls at the bottom of the table to navigate between pages. You can also adjust the number of employees displayed per page using the quantity selector.
