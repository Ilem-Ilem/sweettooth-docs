Organization Management

The Organization Management section allows administrators to organize the company structure by creating and managing department categories and departments. This section helps define the organizational hierarchy and structure.

Accessing Organization Management

  1. Log in to the system with appropriate credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Departments" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section

Understanding the Organizational Structure

The organization management system consists of two main components:

Department Categories:

  • High-level groupings that organize departments by function or purpose
  • Examples: Operations, Sales, Support, Administration, etc.
  • Categories are system-wide and not specific to individual branches

Departments:

  • Specific units within each category that represent actual work areas
  • Departments are typically assigned to specific branches
  • Examples: IT Department, Marketing Department, Customer Service Department

Department Categories Management

Department categories are managed separately from departments:

Accessing Department Categories:

  1. From the main Departments page, click on "Department Categories" in the navigation or submenu
  2. This will take you to the department categories management page

Creating a New Department Category:

  1. On the Department Categories page, click the "Create Category" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface)
  2. Fill in the following required fields:
    • Name - Enter a unique name for the category (e.g., "Operations", "Sales")
    • Description - Provide a detailed description of what this category encompasses
  3. Click the "Save Category" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to create the category

Editing a Department Category:

  1. Find the category you want to edit in the categories list
  2. Click the "Edit" button (the button may appear as text, a pencil icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column for that category
  3. The edit form will load with current values pre-filled
  4. Modify the name or description as needed
  5. Click the "Save Category" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to update the category

Deleting a Department Category:

  1. Find the category you want to delete in the categories list
  2. Click the "Delete" button (the button may appear as text, a trash icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you're sure you want to delete the category
  4. Click "Confirm" to delete the category or "Cancel" to keep it
  5. Note: You cannot delete a category that has departments assigned to it

Departments Management

Departments are managed on the main Departments page:

Creating a New Department:

  1. On the Departments page, click the "Create Department" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface)
  2. Fill in the following required fields:
    • Name - Enter a unique name for the department (e.g., "Marketing Department")
    • Category - Select the appropriate department category from the dropdown
    • Branch - Select which branch this department belongs to (for super admins)
    • Description - Provide a detailed description of the department's function
  3. For regular employees, the branch will be automatically set to their assigned branch
  4. Click the "Save Department" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to create the department

Editing an Existing Department:

  1. Find the department you want to edit in the departments list
  2. Click the "Edit" button (the button may appear as text, a pencil icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column for that department
  3. The edit form will load with current values pre-filled
  4. Modify the name, category, branch, or description as needed
  5. Click the "Save Department" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) to update the department

Deleting a Department:

The deletion process differs based on your user role:

For Super Administrators:
  1. Find the department you want to delete in the departments list
  2. Click the "Delete" button (the button may appear as text, a trash icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you're sure you want to delete the department
  4. Click "Confirm" to immediately delete the department or "Cancel" to keep it
  5. Note: You cannot delete a department that has employees assigned to it
For Regular Employees:
  1. Find the department you want to delete in the departments list
  2. Click the "Delete" button (the button may appear as text, a trash icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  3. A modal will appear asking for a reason for the deletion
  4. Enter a detailed reason (minimum 5 characters required)
  5. Click "Submit" to create an approval request
  6. The request will be sent to a super administrator for review and approval

Approval Workflow

The organization management system implements an approval workflow for non-super-admin users:

For Department Operations:

  • Super Administrators: Can create, edit, and delete departments immediately without approval
  • Regular Employees: Must submit requests for approval when creating, editing, or deleting departments
  • Approval Process: Requests appear in the Audit Management section for super administrators to review
  • Reason Required: Employees must provide a reason for changes (minimum 5 characters)

Reviewing Pending Requests:

  1. Super administrators can view pending requests in the Audit Management section
  2. Navigate to "Audit" → "Approvals" to see all pending requests
  3. Find department-related requests in the list
  4. Click "Approve" or "Reject" to process the request

Filtering and Searching

Both department categories and departments pages offer filtering options:

Department Categories Page:

  • Search - Enter text to search in category names or descriptions
  • Date Range - Filter by creation date range

Departments Page:

  • Search - Enter text to search in department names
  • Advanced Search - Search across multiple fields (name, description)
  • Category Filter - Filter by specific department category
  • Date Range - Filter by creation date range

Bulk Operations

The departments page supports bulk operations:

Bulk Deletion:

  1. Select multiple departments using the checkboxes in the first column of the table
  2. Choose "Delete Selected" from the "Bulk Actions" dropdown
  3. The deletion process follows the same approval workflow as individual deletions
  4. Super administrators can delete multiple departments immediately
  5. Regular employees must provide a reason for bulk deletions
