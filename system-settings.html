System Settings

The System Settings section allows super administrators to configure global system-wide settings that affect the entire SweetTooth application.

Accessing System Settings

  1. Log in to the system with super administrator credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Settings" in the sidebar menu (only visible to super admins)

Settings Tabs Overview

The System Settings page is organized into three main tabs:

  • Business Configuration - Company information, contact details, and backup settings
  • Currency & Localization - Currency settings, date formats, and language preferences
  • Appearance & Theme - Visual customization options and theme settings

Business Configuration Tab

This tab allows you to configure company information and backup settings:

Fields and Controls:

  • Company Name - Enter the name of your business
  • Logo Upload - Upload a logo image (JPG, PNG, max 2MB)
  • Phone Number - Enter your business phone number
  • Email Address - Enter your business email address
  • Auto Backup - Toggle to enable automatic system backups
  • Backup Interval - Set how often backups should occur
  • Backup Period - Set how long to keep backups

Buttons and Actions:

  • Save Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to save all changes made in the form
  • Cancel Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to discard changes and revert to last saved values
  • Remove Logo Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to remove the currently uploaded logo

Currency & Localization Tab

This tab allows you to configure currency and localization settings:

Fields and Controls:

  • Multi-Currency - Toggle to enable support for multiple currencies
  • Primary Currency - Select the primary currency for your business (e.g., USD, EUR, NGN)
  • Default Language - Select the default language for the system
  • Date Format - Select how dates should be displayed (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY)
  • Multi-Tax - Toggle to enable support for multiple tax rates

Buttons and Actions:

  • Save Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to save all changes made in the form
  • Cancel Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to discard changes and revert to last saved values

Appearance & Theme Tab

This tab allows you to customize the visual appearance of the system:

Fields and Controls:

  • Theme Mode - Choose between Light, Dark, or System (follows device settings)
  • Primary Color - Select the primary color for the system theme
  • Sidebar Position - Choose Left or Right for sidebar placement
  • UI Density - Choose Compact, Normal, or Spacious for interface spacing
  • Font Size - Choose Small, Normal, or Large for text size
  • Animation Enabled - Toggle to enable or disable interface animations
  • Light Mode Logo - Upload a logo for light theme
  • Dark Mode Logo - Upload a logo for dark theme

Buttons and Actions:

  • Save Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to save all changes made in the form
  • Cancel Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to discard changes and revert to last saved values
  • Remove Light Mode Logo Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to remove the light mode logo
  • Remove Dark Mode Logo Button - (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) Click to remove the dark mode logo

Switching Between Tabs

To switch between different settings categories:

  1. Look for the tab navigation at the top of the settings page
  2. Click on the tab name you want to view (Business Configuration, Currency & Localization, or Appearance & Theme)
  3. The form fields will change to show the settings for the selected category
  4. Make your changes and save them before switching to another tab

Saving Changes

After making changes to any settings:

  1. Review your changes to ensure they are correct
  2. Click the "Save" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) at the bottom of the form
  3. You will see a success message confirming that changes were saved
  4. If you want to discard changes, click the "Cancel" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface)
