Employee Management

The Employee Management section provides comprehensive tools for managing your workforce, including employee records, attendance tracking, performance evaluations, and HR reporting.

Overview

The Employee Management system is organized into several interconnected modules:

Employee Records

  • All Employees - View and manage employee profiles
  • Create Employee - Add new employees to the system
  • Employee Details - View comprehensive employee information

Attendance Tracking

  • Clock-In Board - Track daily attendance
  • Employee History - View individual attendance records

Performance Management

  • Employee Appraisals - Conduct performance reviews
  • Appraisal Cycles - Manage review periods

HR Reporting

  • Workforce Overview - Comprehensive HR reports

Accessing Employee Management

  1. Log in to the system with appropriate credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Employees" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. Alternatively, access specific modules directly from the navigation

User Roles and Permissions

Different user roles have varying levels of access:

Super Administrators:

  • Full access to all employee management features
  • Can create, edit, and delete employees immediately
  • Can assign roles to employees without approval
  • Can access all branches and departments

HR Managers/Officers:

  • Access to employee records within their assigned branch
  • May need approval for certain operations (depending on configuration)
  • Can conduct appraisals and manage appraisal cycles
  • Can generate HR reports

Regular Employees:

  • Limited access to employee management features
  • May only view their own information
  • May need approval for certain operations

Approval Workflow

Many employee management operations require approval from super administrators:

  • Employee creation requests
  • Employee deletion requests
  • Role assignment changes
  • Employee information updates

When non-super-admin users initiate these operations, they must provide a reason, and the request is sent to super administrators for review and approval.
