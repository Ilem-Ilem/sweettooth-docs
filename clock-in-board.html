Clock-In Board

The Clock-In Board provides comprehensive tracking of employee attendance, including daily clock-ins, historical records, and punctuality analysis.

Overview

The Clock-In Board system consists of multiple views to track employee attendance:

Today's Clock-In Board

View today's attendance with real-time updates

General Clock-In Board

View attendance over a selected date range

Employee History

View individual employee attendance records

Accessing Clock-In Board

  1. Log in to the system with appropriate credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Clock-In Board" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. Choose between "Today" and "All" views from the submenu

Today's Clock-In Board

This view shows all employees who have clocked in today:

Information Displayed:

  • Employee Name - Full name of the employee
  • Employee Number - Unique identifier
  • Department - Department the employee belongs to
  • Shift Type - Morning, afternoon, night, etc.
  • Clock-In Time - Time the employee clocked in
  • Status - On time, early, or late indicator
  • Clock-Out Time - Time the employee clocked out (if applicable)

Statistics Panel:

  • Total Clocked In - Number of employees who have clocked in today
  • Late Count - Number of employees who were late
  • Early Count - Number of employees who were early
  • On-Time Count - Number of employees who were on time

Controls:

  • Department Filter - (dropdown) Filter by specific department
  • Search - (text input) Search by employee name, number, or email
  • Sort By - (dropdown) Sort by clock-in time, name, or department
  • View History - (button) Click to view individual employee's attendance history

General Clock-In Board

This view shows attendance over a customizable date range:

Date Range Selection:

  • Date From - (date picker) Select start date for the range
  • Date To - (date picker) Select end date for the range
  • Default Range - Shows last 7 days by default

Information Displayed:

  • Date - The date of the shift
  • Employee Name - Full name of the employee
  • Employee Number - Unique identifier
  • Department - Department the employee belongs to
  • Shift Type - Morning, afternoon, night, etc.
  • Clock-In Time - Time the employee clocked in
  • Clock-Out Time - Time the employee clocked out (if applicable)
  • Duration - Total hours worked
  • Status - On time, early, or late indicator

Statistics Panel:

  • Total Clocked - Total number of shifts recorded
  • Late Count - Number of late arrivals
  • Early Count - Number of early arrivals
  • On-Time Count - Number of on-time arrivals
  • Total Hours Worked - Sum of all hours worked
  • Late Percentage - Percentage of late arrivals

Controls:

  • Department Filter - (dropdown) Filter by specific department
  • Search - (text input) Search by employee name, number, or email
  • Sort By - (dropdown) Sort by date, name, or department
  • Reset Filters - (button) Reset to default date range (last 7 days)
  • Export CSV - (button) Download attendance data as CSV file

Employee History

This view shows attendance history for a specific employee:

Accessing Employee History:

  1. Navigate to the General Clock-In Board
  2. Click on an employee's name or use the "View History" option
  3. Alternatively, access directly if you have the employee ID

Information Displayed:

  • Date - The date of the shift
  • Clock-In Time - Time the employee clocked in
  • Clock-Out Time - Time the employee clocked out (if applicable)
  • Duration - Total hours worked
  • Shift Type - Morning, afternoon, night, etc.
  • Department - Department during that shift
  • Status - On time, early, or late indicator

Statistics Panel:

  • Total Shifts - Number of shifts worked
  • Total Hours Worked - Sum of all hours worked
  • Average Hours per Shift - Average duration per shift
  • Late Count - Number of late arrivals
  • Early Count - Number of early arrivals
  • On-Time Count - Number of on-time arrivals
  • Late Percentage - Percentage of late arrivals

Controls:

  • Date Range Selection - (date pickers) Select custom date range (default: last 30 days)
  • Department Filter - (dropdown) Filter by specific department
  • Reset Filters - (button) Reset to default date range (last 30 days)
  • Export CSV - (button) Download employee's attendance data as CSV file

Punctuality Status Indicators

The system uses color-coded indicators to show punctuality:

Status Types:

  • Early - Employee arrived before expected time (shown in green)
  • On Time - Employee arrived at expected time (shown in blue)
  • Late - Employee arrived after expected time (shown in red)

Expected Times:

  • Morning Shift - Expected at 06:00
  • Afternoon Shift - Expected at 14:00
  • Night Shift - Expected at 22:00

Exporting Attendance Data

Export attendance data for reporting purposes:

Export Options:

  • General Clock-In Board - Export all attendance data for the selected date range
  • Employee History - Export attendance data for a specific employee

Export Process:

  1. Apply desired filters and date ranges
  2. Click the "Export CSV" button (the button may appear as text, a download icon, or similar depending on your interface)
  3. The system will generate a CSV file with the filtered data
  4. The file will automatically download to your computer

Tips and Best Practices

  • Regularly review attendance patterns to identify trends
  • Use the date range filters to analyze specific periods
  • Export data periodically for external reporting
  • Monitor late arrival percentages for departmental insights
  • Compare individual employee history with department averages
  • Use department filters to evaluate team performance
  • Track total hours worked for payroll preparation
