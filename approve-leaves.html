Approve Leaves

The Approve Leaves page allows managers and HR personnel to review, approve, or reject employee leave applications. This page provides comprehensive tools for managing leave requests efficiently.

Accessing Approve Leaves

  1. Log in to the system with manager or HR credentials
  2. Navigate to the Branch Dashboard
  3. Click on "Leave" in the sidebar menu under the Organization section
  4. Click on "Approve Leaves" from the submenu
  5. The Approve Leaves page will display pending leave applications

Leave Applications List

The page displays a table with the following information for each leave application:

Table Columns:

  • Application # - Unique identifier for the leave application
  • Employee - Name of the employee requesting leave
  • Leave Type - The type of leave requested (e.g., Annual Leave, Sick Leave)
  • Leave Dates - The start and end dates of the requested leave
  • Days - The number of working days requested
  • Status - Current status of the application (Pending, Approved, Rejected, Cancelled)
  • Actions - Available actions for each application

Filtering and Searching

Use the filtering options to find specific leave applications:

Status Filter:

  • Status Dropdown - (dropdown) Filter by specific status (All, Pending, Approved, Rejected, Cancelled)
  • Default view typically shows only "Pending" applications

Search Options:

  • Search Box - (text input) Enter text to search across application numbers, employee names, reasons, and leave type names

Approving Leave Applications

Process for approving a leave application:

Step-by-Step Approval Process:

  1. Find the pending leave application in the list
  2. Click the "View Details" button (the button may appear as text, an eye icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column to review the application
  3. Review the employee's request, dates, and reason
  4. Click the "Approve" button (the button may appear as text, a check icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  5. An approval modal will appear asking for optional approval notes
  6. Enter any relevant notes or comments (optional)
  7. Click the "Approve" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the modal to confirm
  8. The application status will change to "Approved"
  9. The employee will be notified of the approval

Approval Impact:

  • The approved days are deducted from the employee's leave balance
  • The leave is officially recorded in the system
  • The employee's calendar may be updated to reflect the approved leave

Rejecting Leave Applications

Process for rejecting a leave application:

Step-by-Step Rejection Process:

  1. Find the pending leave application in the list
  2. Click the "View Details" button (the button may appear as text, an eye icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column to review the application
  3. Review the employee's request, dates, and reason
  4. Click the "Reject" button (the button may appear as text, an X icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the Actions column
  5. A rejection modal will appear requiring a reason for the rejection
  6. Enter a detailed reason for rejection (minimum 10 characters required)
  7. Click the "Reject" button (the button may appear as text, an icon, or similar depending on your interface) in the modal to confirm
  8. The application status will change to "Rejected"
  9. The employee will be notified of the rejection with the provided reason

Rejection Impact:

  • The requested days are returned to the employee's leave balance
  • The employee can submit a new request if needed
  • The rejection is recorded in the system for audit purposes

Leave Details Modal

When viewing leave details, you'll see comprehensive information:

Application Information:

  • Application Number - Unique identifier for the application
  • Employee Name - Full name of the requesting employee
  • Employee ID - Employee number or ID
  • Leave Type - The type of leave requested
  • Dates - Start and end dates of the requested leave
  • Total Days - Number of working days requested
  • Reason - Explanation provided by the employee
  • Emergency Contact - Contact information provided for emergencies

Supporting Documents:

  • If a supporting document was uploaded, a "Download Document" button (the button may appear as text, a download icon, or similar depending on your interface) will be available
  • Click the button to download the document to your computer
  • Review documents to validate the leave request if required

Approval/Rejection Options:

  • Modal provides both "Approve" and "Reject" buttons for quick decision-making
  • Additional notes can be added during the approval or rejection process

Bulk Operations

Perform actions on multiple leave applications simultaneously:

Selecting Multiple Applications:

  • Check the checkbox in the first column of each application row you want to select
  • Or use the "Select All" checkbox to select all applications on the current page

Bulk Actions:

  • Currently, bulk operations may be limited to viewing or exporting data
  • Individual approval/rejection is recommended to ensure proper review of each application

Pagination

The leave applications list is divided into pages to improve performance. Use the pagination controls at the bottom of the table to navigate between pages. You can also adjust the number of applications displayed per page using the quantity selector.

Approval Considerations

Factors to consider when approving or rejecting leave applications:

Business Needs:

  • Ensure adequate coverage during the requested period
  • Consider peak business periods or critical project timelines
  • Check for overlapping leave requests from team members
  • Evaluate the timing in relation to business objectives

Employee Factors:

  • Review the employee's leave history and patterns
  • Consider the employee's performance and reliability
  • Evaluate the validity of the reason provided
  • Take into account personal circumstances if applicable

Policy Compliance:

  • Verify the request complies with company leave policies
  • Check that the requested days don't exceed available balance
  • Ensure proper notice was given if required
  • Confirm required documentation is provided if needed

Tips and Best Practices

  • Review leave applications promptly to avoid delays
  • Provide clear reasons when rejecting applications
  • Consider team dynamics when approving leave requests
  • Keep detailed records of approval decisions
  • Communicate approval decisions clearly to employees
  • Plan ahead for periods with multiple leave requests
  • Ensure equitable treatment of all employees' requests
  • Stay informed about company leave policies and updates
  • Coordinate with other managers for cross-team coverage
  • Use the search and filter functions to efficiently manage applications
